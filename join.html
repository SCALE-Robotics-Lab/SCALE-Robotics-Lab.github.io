Join the Lab

We are always looking for technically strong, self-motivated students to join our team. Our research focuses on advancing machine learning and artificial intelligence to improve robot learning, human-robot interaction, and multi-agent collaboration.

Prospective Ph.D. Students

If you are applying for the upcoming cycle, please apply directly to the Purdue Computer Science Ph.D. program and mention my name in your application.

Please email me prior to applying with:

  • A brief introduction & research interests
  • Your CV / Resume
  • Links to relevant publications or projects

Admitted Ph.D. Students

If you have already been admitted to Purdue and are looking for an advisor, please email me directly to set up a meeting.

I am able to directly advise students in the CS department and can co-advise students in other departments (e.g., ME, ECE).

Purdue MS & UG Students

If you are currently at Purdue and are passionate about robotics and AI, we would love to hear from you.

Please email me with:

  • Subject: [Prospective MS/UG Student]
  • Your CV / Resume
  • A brief description of your interest in the SCALE Lab
